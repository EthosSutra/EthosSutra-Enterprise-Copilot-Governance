# Vendor Assessment

## Purpose

The Vendor Assessment process establishes the framework for evaluating, approving, monitoring and managing third-party vendors supporting the EthosSutra Enterprise Copilot.

The objective is to ensure that vendors operate in a manner consistent with governance, security, privacy, compliance and operational resilience requirements.

Vendor assessments support risk-based decision making and help ensure that third-party dependencies do not introduce unacceptable risks to the organization.

## Key Outcomes

* Vendor risks identified
* Third-party controls assessed
* Security posture evaluated
* Compliance risks reviewed
* Operational resilience assessed
* Vendor approval decisions documented

## Governance Objectives

The Vendor Assessment process supports:

* Third-party risk management
* Responsible AI practices
* Regulatory compliance
* Security assurance
* Operational resilience
* Governance oversight

## Vendor Risk Domains

Vendor assessments should evaluate:

* Information Security
* Privacy and Data Protection
* AI Governance
* Regulatory Compliance
* Operational Resilience
* Financial Stability
* Service Reliability
* Subprocessor Management

## Vendor Monitoring

Approved vendors should be subject to periodic review and ongoing monitoring.

Monitoring activities may include:

* Security reviews
* Incident reviews
* Compliance reviews
* Service availability monitoring
* Contract reviews
