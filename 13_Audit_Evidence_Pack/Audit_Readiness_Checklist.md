# Audit Readiness Checklist

## Purpose

This checklist is used to assess the audit readiness of the EthosSutra Enterprise Copilot governance programme.

The objective is to verify that governance controls, documentation, evidence and operational activities are sufficiently established to support internal audits, external assessments and regulatory reviews.

This checklist should be completed periodically and prior to formal audit activities.

## Auditor Validation Questions

The following questions should be answerable during an audit:

* Who owns the AI system?
* What risks have been identified?
* What controls mitigate those risks?
* How is the system monitored?
* How are incidents managed?
* How is human oversight maintained?
* What evidence demonstrates control effectiveness?
* How does the system align with NIST AI RMF, ISO 42001 and the EU AI Act?
