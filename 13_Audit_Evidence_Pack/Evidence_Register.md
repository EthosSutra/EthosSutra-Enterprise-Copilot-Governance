# Evidence Register

## Purpose

The Evidence Register provides a centralized inventory of governance evidence supporting the operation of the EthosSutra Enterprise Copilot.

The objective is to demonstrate that governance controls are not only documented but are actively implemented, monitored and maintained throughout the AI system lifecycle.

This register serves as the primary reference for audit activities, governance reviews and compliance assessments.

## Evidence Management Principles

Evidence should be:

* Accurate
* Complete
* Traceable
* Current
* Retained appropriately
* Accessible to authorized reviewers

Evidence should support governance, risk management, compliance and operational assurance activities.

## Evidence Quality Criteria

Evidence should satisfy the following requirements:

### Completeness

Evidence demonstrates full execution of governance activities.

### Accuracy

Evidence reflects actual governance activities performed.

### Traceability

Evidence can be linked to controls, risks and governance requirements.

### Timeliness

Evidence remains current and reflects operational reality.

### Retention

Evidence is maintained according to retention requirements.
