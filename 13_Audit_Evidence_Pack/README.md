# Audit Evidence Pack

## Purpose

The Audit Evidence Pack provides a structured collection of evidence demonstrating that governance controls for the EthosSutra Enterprise Copilot have been implemented, operated and monitored.

The objective is to support internal reviews, external audits, regulatory assessments and governance assurance activities.

The Audit Evidence Pack serves as the primary mechanism for demonstrating that governance requirements are not only documented but are actively operating.

## Evidence Principles

### Traceability

Evidence should be traceable to governance controls and risk management activities.

### Completeness

Evidence should demonstrate that governance activities are operating effectively.

### Accuracy

Evidence should reflect actual governance activities and outcomes.

### Retention

Evidence should be retained according to governance and compliance requirements.

### Accessibility

Evidence should be available for authorized review when required.
