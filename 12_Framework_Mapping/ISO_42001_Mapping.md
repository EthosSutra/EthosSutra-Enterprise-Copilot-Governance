# ISO 42001 Mapping

## Purpose

This document maps the governance controls implemented for the EthosSutra Enterprise Copilot to ISO/IEC 42001:2023 Artificial Intelligence Management System (AIMS) requirements.

The objective is to demonstrate how governance controls, risk management activities and operational processes support responsible AI management practices.

This mapping supports governance assurance, audit readiness and alignment with internationally recognized AI governance standards.

## ISO 42001 Overview

ISO/IEC 42001 establishes requirements for an Artificial Intelligence Management System (AIMS).

The standard focuses on:

* Governance and Leadership
* Risk Management
* Operational Controls
* AI Lifecycle Management
* Monitoring and Improvement
* Accountability and Oversight

The EthosSutra Enterprise Copilot governance framework incorporates controls supporting these objectives.
