The information collected through this intake process supports governance review, risk assessment, inventory registration, compliance evaluation and approval decisions.

# Business Justification

## Business Need

Organizations increasingly require guidance on AI governance, risk management, compliance obligations and responsible AI practices.

The EthosSutra Enterprise Copilot is intended to provide governance-focused support through conversational interactions powered by generative AI technology.

---

## Expected Business Outcomes

* Improve governance awareness
* Increase accessibility of governance knowledge
* Support governance documentation activities
* Improve consistency of governance guidance
* Promote responsible AI adoption
* Support audit readiness initiatives
