# Acceptable Use Policy

## Purpose

The purpose of this policy is to establish acceptable usage requirements for the EthosSutra Enterprise Copilot.

This policy helps ensure that the AI system is used responsibly, securely and in accordance with governance, compliance and ethical requirements.

All users are responsible for complying with this policy when interacting with the system.

---

# Scope

This policy applies to:

- Employees
- Contractors
- Governance professionals
- Compliance personnel
- Auditors
- Authorized users

This policy applies to all interactions with the EthosSutra Enterprise Copilot.

---

# Permitted Uses

The system may be used for:

- AI governance guidance
- Risk management support
- Governance documentation assistance
- Compliance awareness activities
- Framework interpretation support
- Audit preparation assistance
- Governance research and learning

Users should verify AI-generated outputs before relying on them.

---

# Prohibited Uses

Users must not use the system for:

## Unauthorized Data Processing

- Uploading confidential information without authorization
- Uploading sensitive personal information
- Uploading regulated data without approval

---

## Harmful Activities

- Generating malicious content
- Circumventing security controls
- Attempting prompt injection attacks
- Testing system vulnerabilities without authorization

---

## Decision Automation

The system must not be used as the sole basis for:

- Regulatory decisions
- Compliance approvals
- Risk acceptance decisions
- Audit conclusions
- Legal determinations

Human review is required.

---

## Misrepresentation

Users must not:

- Present AI-generated content as verified fact without review
- Misrepresent AI-generated outputs as official organizational positions
- Use AI-generated content without validation where accuracy is required

---

# User Responsibilities

Users are responsible for:

- Following governance requirements
- Protecting organizational information
- Reviewing AI-generated outputs
- Reporting incidents and concerns
- Escalating suspected inaccuracies

---

# Human Review Requirements

Human review is mandatory when outputs are used for:

- Governance recommendations
- Compliance activities
- Risk assessments
- Audit evidence
- Management reporting

Users remain accountable for decisions supported by the AI system.

---

# Security Requirements

Users must:

- Protect authentication credentials
- Follow access management requirements
- Report suspected security incidents
- Avoid sharing restricted information

---

# Monitoring

System usage may be monitored for:

- Security purposes
- Governance oversight
- Incident investigation
- Compliance review
- Performance monitoring

Monitoring activities should comply with applicable policies and standards.

---

# Violations

Violations of this policy may result in:

- Removal of access
- Governance review
- Corrective actions
- Incident investigation

---

# Exceptions

Exceptions must be approved by the AI Governance Lead and documented through the governance process.
