# Governance Policies

## Purpose

The Governance Policies folder contains the foundational policies and standards that govern the development, deployment, operation and monitoring of the EthosSutra Enterprise Copilot.

These policies establish management expectations, define acceptable practices and provide governance controls necessary to support responsible AI use.

The policies serve as the primary mechanism for translating governance principles into operational requirements.

AI systems must be governed through documented policies, clearly defined responsibilities and effective control mechanisms.

No AI system should operate outside approved governance requirements.
