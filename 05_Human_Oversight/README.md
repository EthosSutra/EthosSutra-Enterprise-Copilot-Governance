# Human Oversight

## Purpose

The Human Oversight process establishes accountability, review responsibilities and decision-making authority for the EthosSutra Enterprise Copilot.

The objective is to ensure that AI-generated outputs remain subject to appropriate human judgment, review and intervention throughout the system lifecycle.

Human oversight is a foundational governance control that helps reduce risks associated with inaccurate outputs, inappropriate recommendations, automation bias and operational failures.

## Human Oversight Principles

### Human Accountability

Humans remain accountable for decisions supported by AI systems.

### Human Review

AI-generated outputs must be reviewed when used for governance, compliance, risk management or audit-related activities.

### Human Intervention

Authorized individuals must be able to intervene, override or suspend AI-supported processes when required.

### Escalation

Issues identified through oversight activities should be escalated through defined governance channels.
