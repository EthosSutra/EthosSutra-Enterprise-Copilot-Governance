# # AI System Inventory

## Purpose

The AI System Inventory serves as the central registry of AI systems governed under the EthosSutra Enterprise Copilot Governance Program.

Maintaining an accurate inventory is a foundational governance requirement and enables visibility, accountability, risk management and compliance oversight throughout the AI system lifecycle.

## Governance Principle

If an AI system is not included within the inventory, it is considered outside the formal governance program and cannot proceed through governance review, risk assessment or production approval.
