# AI System Register

## Purpose

The AI System Register serves as the authoritative inventory record for AI systems governed under the EthosSutra Enterprise Copilot Governance Program.

The register provides visibility into system ownership, technical architecture, risk classification, governance obligations and lifecycle status.

# Human Oversight Requirements

Human oversight is mandatory for:

* Governance recommendations
* Compliance interpretations
* Risk management decisions
* Audit-related conclusions
* Control implementation decisions

The system is designed to support human decision-making and does not replace human judgment.
