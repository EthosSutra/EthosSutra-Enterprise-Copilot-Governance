# Incident Management

## Purpose

The Incident Management process establishes the framework for identifying, reporting, assessing, responding to and resolving incidents associated with the EthosSutra Enterprise Copilot.

The objective is to minimize operational, compliance, security and governance impacts arising from AI-related incidents while ensuring appropriate escalation, accountability and corrective action.

Incident management supports responsible AI practices and provides assurance that significant issues are handled consistently and effectively.

## Incident Management Principles

### Early Detection

Potential incidents should be identified and reported as early as possible.

### Risk-Based Response

Response activities should be proportional to the severity and impact of the incident.

### Accountability

Roles and responsibilities should be clearly defined for incident management activities.

### Documentation

All incidents should be documented to support governance oversight and audit requirements.

### Continuous Improvement

Lessons learned should be incorporated into governance processes and controls.

## Incident Types

The following categories may require incident management activities:

### AI Output Incidents

Examples:

* Hallucinations
* Incorrect recommendations
* Misleading responses
* Governance inaccuracies

### Security Incidents

Examples:

* Unauthorized access
* Credential compromise
* Data exposure
* Prompt injection attacks

### Compliance Incidents

Examples:

* Policy violations
* Regulatory breaches
* Governance control failures

### Operational Incidents

Examples:

* Service outages
* Integration failures
* Retrieval failures
* Vendor disruptions

### Vendor Incidents

Examples:

* Third-party service outages
* Provider security incidents
* Vendor compliance concerns

## Incident Severity Levels

| Severity | Description                                                       |
| -------- | ----------------------------------------------------------------- |
| Critical | Significant legal, regulatory, operational or reputational impact |
| High     | Major governance, compliance or business impact                   |
| Medium   | Manageable impact requiring corrective action                     |
| Low      | Minor issue with limited operational impact                       |

Severity ratings guide escalation and response activities.
