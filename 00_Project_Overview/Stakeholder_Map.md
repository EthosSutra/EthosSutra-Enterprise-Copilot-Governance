# Stakeholder Map

## Purpose

This document identifies stakeholders involved in the governance, operation, oversight and assurance activities associated with the EthosSutra Enterprise Copilot.

Understanding stakeholder responsibilities is essential for establishing accountability, implementing governance controls and ensuring effective oversight throughout the AI system lifecycle.

---

## AI System

**System Name:** EthosSutra Enterprise Copilot

**System Type:** Generative AI Assistant

**Business Function:** AI Governance, Compliance and Risk Management Support

## Stakeholder Roles

### Executive Sponsor

#### Purpose

Provides strategic direction and organizational support for AI governance activities.

#### Responsibilities

* Support governance initiatives
* Review governance performance
* Ensure governance objectives align with business goals
* Promote responsible AI practices

---

### AI Governance Lead

#### Purpose

Owns and manages the AI governance program.

#### Responsibilities

* Maintain governance framework
* Coordinate governance activities
* Review governance controls
* Oversee compliance activities
* Support audit readiness
* Drive continuous improvement

---

### AI System Owner

#### Purpose

Accountable for day-to-day operation of the AI system.

#### Responsibilities

* Ensure system operates as intended
* Maintain system documentation
* Coordinate testing activities
* Escalate governance issues
* Support monitoring activities

---

### Risk Management Lead

#### Purpose

Responsible for identifying, assessing and monitoring AI-related risks.

#### Responsibilities

* Conduct risk assessments
* Maintain risk register
* Monitor risk treatment activities
* Review emerging risks
* Support incident investigations

---

### Compliance Lead

#### Purpose

Ensures governance activities align with applicable frameworks and regulatory expectations.

#### Responsibilities

* Review compliance obligations
* Monitor regulatory developments
* Support framework mapping activities
* Maintain compliance evidence
* Participate in governance reviews

---

### End Users

#### Purpose

Utilize the EthosSutra Enterprise Copilot for governance-related support.

#### Responsibilities

* Use the system responsibly
* Follow acceptable use requirements
* Report issues and incidents
* Provide feedback on system performance

---

### Internal Audit

#### Purpose

Provides independent assurance regarding governance effectiveness.

#### Responsibilities

* Evaluate governance controls
* Review evidence and documentation
* Assess audit readiness
* Identify control gaps
* Provide recommendations for improvement

## Governance Reporting Structure

The AI Governance Lead serves as the central coordination point for governance activities and provides oversight across risk management, compliance, operational monitoring and audit readiness functions.

Material governance issues, significant risks and critical incidents are escalated to the Executive Sponsor for review and decision-making.
