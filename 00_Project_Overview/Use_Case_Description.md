## Business Purpose

The EthosSutra Enterprise Copilot is a generative AI assistant designed to provide guidance and support on topics related to AI governance, compliance, risk management and responsible AI practices.

The system is intended to help users understand governance concepts, navigate governance frameworks and improve governance-related decision-making through conversational interactions.

---

## Business Problem

Organizations adopting artificial intelligence frequently encounter challenges related to governance, accountability, risk management and regulatory compliance.

Common challenges include:

* Limited governance expertise
* Inconsistent documentation practices
* Unclear ownership structures
* Difficulty interpreting governance frameworks
* Limited visibility into governance obligations
* Lack of audit-ready evidence

The EthosSutra Enterprise Copilot is intended to address these challenges by providing accessible governance-related guidance and support.

---

## Intended Users

The primary users of the system include:

* AI Governance Professionals
* AI Risk Managers
* Compliance Specialists
* Internal Auditors
* Data Governance Practitioners
* Responsible AI Practitioners
* Students and Learners

---

## Intended Capabilities

The system is expected to:

* Explain AI governance concepts
* Provide governance guidance
* Support risk management activities
* Assist with governance documentation
* Explain compliance requirements
* Support governance framework understanding
* Provide governance best practices

---

## Expected Benefits

Implementation of the EthosSutra Enterprise Copilot is expected to provide:

* Improved governance awareness
* Increased access to governance knowledge
* Consistent governance guidance
* Better understanding of governance frameworks
* Improved governance documentation quality
* Enhanced audit readiness

---

## Data Inputs

The system may process:

* User prompts
* Governance-related questions
* Governance documentation content
* Framework-related information
* User feedback

The system is not intended to process highly sensitive personal information or regulated data.

---

## Data Outputs

The system may generate:

* Governance recommendations
* Governance explanations
* Risk management guidance
* Documentation support
* Compliance-related information
* Educational content

Generated outputs should be reviewed by users before being relied upon for business decisions.

---

## Human Oversight Requirements

Human oversight is required to:

* Review generated responses
* Validate governance recommendations
* Verify compliance interpretations
* Confirm risk-related decisions
* Assess output quality

Users remain responsible for decisions made using information provided by the system.

---

## Key Risks

Potential risks include:

* Inaccurate information
* Hallucinated responses
* Misinterpretation of regulatory requirements
* Outdated governance guidance
* Overreliance on generated outputs

These risks will be managed through governance controls, testing, monitoring and oversight activities.

---

## Success Criteria

The use case will be considered successful when:

* Users receive relevant governance guidance
* Responses remain accurate and understandable
* Governance documentation supports audit readiness
* Risks are identified and managed appropriately
* Governance controls operate effectively
* Monitoring activities demonstrate ongoing oversight
