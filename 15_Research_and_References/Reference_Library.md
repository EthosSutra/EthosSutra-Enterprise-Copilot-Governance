# Reference Library

## Purpose

This document serves as the central repository of external references, standards, regulations, guidance documents and research materials used during the development of the EthosSutra Enterprise Copilot governance framework.

The objective is to provide traceability to authoritative sources supporting governance decisions, control design, framework mappings and risk management activities.

This library should be periodically reviewed and updated as standards, regulations and industry practices evolve.

## Governance Standards and Frameworks
