# KPI Definitions

## Purpose

This document defines the Key Performance Indicators (KPIs) and Key Risk Indicators (KRIs) used to monitor the performance, effectiveness and governance of the EthosSutra Enterprise Copilot.

The objective is to provide measurable indicators that support governance oversight, risk management and continuous improvement.

## KPI Governance Principles

All KPIs should be:

* Measurable
* Repeatable
* Actionable
* Risk-based
* Aligned with governance objectives

Metrics should be reviewed regularly and used to support governance decision-making.

## Governance Performance KPIs

### Governance Accuracy Score

**Objective**

Measure the accuracy of governance-related responses.

| Metric Attribute | Value              |
| ---------------- | ------------------ |
| KPI ID           | KPI-001            |
| Target           | ≥ 95%              |
| Frequency        | Monthly            |
| Owner            | AI Governance Lead |

**Calculation**

(Number of Accurate Responses ÷ Total Responses Reviewed) × 100
